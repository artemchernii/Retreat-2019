Activities

The 3 activities organized have as purpose to provide you with moments of conviviality and union with your colleagues, so that you can share experiences that, in a work environment, are not always possible. Your participation is optional.
The edition#1 will be composed of 5 non-formal talks, with a duration of 12 minutes each. The speakers are going to talk about topics - tech or non-tech – of their domain, that they would like to share with the colleagues. The objective of these Light Talks is to create moments so that each one can explore topics of their interest and knowledge.
THROUGH THE CITY BY SKATE
Marcelo Fortunato

THROUGH THE CITY BY SKATE

The handrail is only useful to ensure the safety of who uses it? Stairs are only useful to move to another level? For a skater, they are not!
Marcelo is 30 years old, married, web developer and fond of extreme sports, mainly skateboard! Since he had 13 years old, he practices and follows skateboard.
FINANCE TRICKS AND TIPS FOR WHO HAS NOW STARTED WORK
Diogo da Silva

FINANCE TRICKS AND TIPS FOR WHO HAS NOW STARTED WORK

Small finance tips for who has now started work, from alternatives to a savings account to very basic principles of investment. At the end of this conversation, we hope that you have more interest in knowing what is: inflation, savings, raize and compound interest.
Diogo is 27 years old and he is a fullstack developer at PDMFC and interested in the savings area. At 18 years old, his grandfather gave him a savings account that was created when he was born. After seeing the almost null income, he started this quest to understand how to save and invest effectively.
THE KEY MILESTONES AND PHOTOGRAFERS OF THE PHOTOGRAPHY HISTORY
Marco Pereira

THE KEY MILESTONES AND PHOTOGRAFERS OF THE PHOTOGRAPHY HISTORY

Despite the arduous task of trying to summarize a secular history in only 10 minutes of lecture, this conviviality will not be more than a unilateral conversation where the speaker will try to expose the key milestones of the history of photography, as well as to introduce the photographers that most influenced the photographic techniques and fields that we know nowadays.
Graduate, postgraduate and master in Communication, Multimedia and Marketing. At 21 years old, he started his professional journey in the media area and in parallel in the world of arts. He has participated in national and international film festivals, with films of his authorship. Besides being a musician, marketeer and filmmaker, he is also a photographer with training in Analogue Photography.
BACK TO THE SIXTIES
Nuno Pedrosa

BACK TO THE SIXTIES

While most of the world has the benefit of using the metric system, a few old habits still remain. We still buy some items in dozens, we still tell the time using weird values. Why is that?
Nuno has been in PDMFC since 2002, dabbling mostly in programming. He has dealt with a boatload of programming languages, outside of the MS world. He enjoys mapping, navigation, photography and mostly being outdoors, be it on two feet, two wheels or four AWD wheels. He also enjoys a lot of the side tidbits that go along with the main interests, so beware that you may find him somewhat boring if he engages on speaking of medieval torture or other key subjects…
ABOUT THE TRUTH OF FAKE NEWS
Rui Campos

ABOUT THE TRUTH OF FAKE NEWS

What are news and what are not news. What is the truth and what is the lie. The difference between truth, half-truths, half-lies and lies. The admirable new world of the rumour to social media.
Rui Campos is one of the founders of PDMFC, known for the sentence "I am the most important because I am the one who pays your wage!”. Economist and freethinker, it is one of the most important philosophers in the world of the Neo Socratic school -"And now... something completely different!"
The Docs that will be displayed will allow to reflect about questions related to information sharing and internet trends, human and minority rights, among others. The purpose of the Docs is to create a relaxed and entertaining atmosphere, instigating creativity. This session, with a duration of 1h30, will be composed of a range of documentaries, which will be revealed on March 15.
